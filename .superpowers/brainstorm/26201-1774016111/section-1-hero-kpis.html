Design Decisions

  • 3 hero cards — Omzet, Winst, Belasting. These are THE numbers. Large (32px), with sparklines.
  • Sparklines — 12-month trend lines inside Omzet and Winst cards. Instant pattern recognition without a full chart.
  • Simplified Belasting card — one progress bar + two values. No more embedded dialog (VA entry moves to Aangifte page).
  • YoY comparison — small pill badge (↑11%) + "vs vorig jaar" subtext. Context without clutter.
  • Secondary strip — Uren, Km, Documenten become compact horizontal bars. Half the height of hero cards. Only shown when meaningful.
  • Quick actions — moved to the header row next to year selector. No longer floating awkwardly between sections.
  • Rounded values in heroes — € 28.702 not € 28.702,52. Precision is for detail pages.
  • Bedrijfslasten card removed — it's redundant (Winst = Omzet - Lasten). Visible in the cost chart below.
