Design Decisions

  • Charts: 3:2 ratio — revenue chart gets more width (it's the primary trend), cost donut is supplementary. Not 50/50.
  • Revenue chart: teal vs light gray — current year pops, previous year recedes. No competing colors.
  • Cost donut: teal palette — monochromatic teal shades instead of rainbow. Stays cohesive with brand. Legend shows actual amounts.
  • Empty cost chart — when no expenses exist (like 2026 currently), the entire donut section shows a purposeful empty state instead of "Geen uitgaven gevonden."
  • Aandachtspunten = contextual alerts — only appear when something needs attention. Warm amber/orange tones signal "action needed" without alarm. Each alert is a single line with count + amount + "Bekijk" link.
  • When nothing needs attention — the entire Aandachtspunten section disappears. No "0 openstaand" cards.
  • Section label — small uppercase "AANDACHTSPUNTEN" gives visual structure without a heavy heading.
