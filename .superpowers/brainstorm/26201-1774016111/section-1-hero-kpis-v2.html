What changed from v1

  • Material Icons — proper material-icons-round font, same as the actual NiceGUI app. No emojis.
  • Icons only in secondary strip — hero cards have no icons at all. The number IS the content. Icons in heroes were visual noise.
  • Tighter spacing — less padding, less margin. Dense but breathable.
  • Buttons refined — white background, subtle border, teal "+" icon. Blends with the header instead of floating.
  • No uppercase labels — the hero card labels use normal casing. Calmer.
