Feature: Real VA Bank Matching

The Belasting prognose card currently fakes "VA betaald" via annual_va × month ÷ 12. The redesign will:

  • Scan banktransacties for Belastingdienst payments (by IBAN/description)
  • Categorize as IB or ZVW and sum actual payments
  • Show "3 van 12 termijnen ontvangen" with real amounts
  • Fall back to proration with "geschat" label only when no bank matches exist

Removed / Changed

  • Bedrijfslasten card — removed (redundant: Winst = Omzet − Lasten)
  • VA dialog on dashboard — moved to Aangifte page
  • "Openstaand: 0" — hidden when zero (shown as alert only when > 0)
  • Icons on hero cards — removed (the number is the hero)
  • Empty chart states — purposeful message or section hidden entirely
